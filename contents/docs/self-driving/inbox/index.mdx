The inbox is where self-driving hands work back to you. [Reports](/docs/self-driving/reports) and the pull requests the loop opens both land here, ranked by priority, so the most important work rises to the top.

It's the human side of the [self-improving loop](/docs/self-driving/self-improving-loop): the loop watches, enriches, and proposes, and the inbox is where you review, steer, and decide.

## What lands in your inbox

- **Reports** – grouped [signals](/docs/self-driving/signals) framed as one problem, with the evidence behind them.
- **Pull requests** – the changes an agent opens for actionable reports.

Each item is ranked by priority, so you work top-down instead of triaging a flat list.

## What you do here

From the inbox you:

- **Review** a report and dig into the evidence.
- **Steer** the work: approve it, snooze it, or decline what self-driving proposes.
- **Merge** the pull requests you're happy with, the same as any other PR.

Nothing ships without you. The inbox is where you stay in control of what self-driving does.

## Where to find it

The inbox is available across surfaces — the [web app](/docs/self-driving/web), [PostHog Code](/docs/posthog-code), and Slack — so you can review and act on work wherever you are. It's the same inbox everywhere; only the way you open it changes.

To start filling it, connect your [signal sources](/docs/self-driving/inbox/sources).
