## Q3 2026 objectives

### Objective 1: Semantic layer (owner: Jovan)

**Motivation:** We are increasingly hearing about inconsistency issues when an agent writes a query or an insight based on its assumptions around a definition. Same problem of "what's the source of truth?" like we humans have, but agent-amplified.

**What we'll ship:**
- A central semantic layer for all metrics and definitions parts of PostHog that need to do analysis or generate queries on top of a user's shared business understanding

### Objective 2: Integration with Managed warehouse (owner: Jovan)

**Motivation:** We need to move workloads away from ClickHouse and onto the managed warehouse — modeling and endpoints included.

**What we'll ship:**
- Endpoints execution running solely on the managed warehouse with appropriate compute control
- Move materialization jobs to the managed warehouse, and limit usage on CH
- Support pricing and billing instrumentation

### Objective 3: Version control (owner: New hire)

**Motivation:** While data modeling can happen inside PostHog, lack of version control makes it difficult to collaborate and safely make changes.

**What we'll ship:**
- An easy way for agents and humans to interact with their git-based data modeling project
- And enable a development workflow that doesn't impact the production models

### Objective 4: Testing (owner: Jovan & New hire)

**Motivation:** Our agent's SQL writing has significantly improved, but we still have the verifiability gap where the users do not have a way of checking whether the models they build act as intended.

**What we'll ship:**
- Schema and data tests for data models
- That are integrated into the version control workflow
- And easily used by agents

### Side quest 1 (owner: Anna)

**Motivation:** Not every customer has custom and complex data modeling needs. Some are very standard and can be templated into models that take a customer from 0 to 1 in modeling their revenue / active users / attribution.

**What we'll ship:**
- A set of skills that provide all the context to an agent around what data models it needs to create in PostHog and how to verify them

### Side quest 2 (owner: Anna)

**Motivation:** We need to stay on top of the data space and how it's evolving. A lot has happened recently, and we want to make sure that what we're building is aligned with the field.

**What we'll do:**
- Introduce recurring data space wrap-up research automation
